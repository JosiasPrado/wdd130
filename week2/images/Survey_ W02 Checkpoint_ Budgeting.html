Started: Jan 20 at 2:38pm

Quiz Instructions

Overview

  • Purpose: Practice with budgeting and get feedback by comparing your work to a key.
  • Task: Complete the spreadsheet, check your work, and take the quiz.

Part 1: Complete the Spreadsheet

  1. Download these documents:
    • W02 Checkpoint: Budgeting Word document Download W02 Checkpoint: Budgeting Word document
    • W02 Checkpoint: Budgeting Excel document Download W02 Checkpoint: Budgeting Excel document
  2. Open both documents and complete the assignment by following the directions in the Word document.

Part 2: Check Your Work

  1. Download W02 Checkpoints: Budget KEY Download W02 Checkpoints: Budget KEY and check your work.
  2. If you find a mistake, figure out what your mistake was and fix it.
  3. If you need help fixing your mistakes reach out to one of these resources:
    • Your WhatsApp group
    • Online Tutoring Links to an external site.
    • Your Instructor

Part 3: Take Quiz

  1. After you have completed the spreadsheet, take the quiz. 
  2. You will submit your spreadsheet as part of the quiz. This is just to verify that the spreadsheet was completed. The graders will not be giving you feedback on your spreadsheet.
